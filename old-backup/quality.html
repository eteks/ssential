Quality

Quality and value for money has been the major key elements in the products ever since the 1950s. Products are known for its quality and prices are believed to be more than satisfactory for the customers. Every product is manufactured with natural materials and wood dust as base. In todays world, when people can devote hardly any time for prayer and Pooja, it is even more challenging to get some time to buy those essential items to perform Pooja during festivals. Many of us wouldn't even know if we have all items to perform a complete Pooja. Many a times we realise that we have missed out on few items after we have started the Pooja.

Pooja Essentials is created just to fulfill this need, to help every one perform a complete Pooja and derive the satisfaction of receiving God's blessings.
Pooja Essentials has come out with this concept of putting together those items in the form of a kit designed by experts having 40 years of experience in helping people perform Pooja during festivals.

The famous Iyer Sandal Shop ( Iyer Sandana Kadai) having rich experience in manufacturing agar bathis based out of Trichy would supply the kits. Pooja essentials has its umbilical cord.connection with Iyyer Sandal Shop and will reach out to people who want to perform a complete Pooja during Hindu festivals.
The kit would contain products "Chamundeshwari" the brand known for its quality, safety and value for money since the 1970s.
